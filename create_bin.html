Create the pledges bin

You have two options. Pick whichever is faster.

Option A · One-click (needs JSONBin Master Key)

Grab your Master Key from jsonbin.io/api-keys, paste it below, and click the button. This provisions the bin and prints the ID.

Pre-filled. Click the button to create the bin.

Option B · Manual (30 seconds, no key needed)

  1. Go to jsonbin.io/app/bins and sign in.
  2. Click Create Bin.
  3. Paste this into the editor: 
    [{"_seed": true}]
  4. Set Private to OFF (so the reveal page can read it without an access key).
  5. Click Create. The URL will look like jsonbin.io/app/bins/abc123... — the part after /bins/ is your bin ID.
  6. Paste that ID into index.html where it says REPLACE_WITH_PLEDGES_BIN_ID.
